Manufacturing Process & Heat Treatment of SS 310

Annealing

The annealing process for SS 310 involves heating the material to a temperature range of 1050°C to 1150°C, followed by a controlled cooling process. This treatment helps to relieve internal stresses, improve ductility, and enhance the overall microstructure of the alloy, making it suitable for further processing and use in various applications.

Forging

Forging of SS 310 is performed at elevated temperatures, typically between 1000°C and 1200°C. This process involves shaping the metal through compressive forces, which enhances its mechanical properties and ensures a fine-grained structure. Forging is crucial for producing strong and durable nut bolt components that can withstand high-temperature environments.
