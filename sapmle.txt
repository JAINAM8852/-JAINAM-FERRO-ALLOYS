## 5. Applications and Industries

Our aluminum sheets are the material of choice for critical applications across various sectors:

*   **Automotive:** Body panels, heat shields, structural components (e.g., 6061-T6).
*   **Marine & Shipbuilding:** Hull construction, superstructures, and internal fittings (e.g., 5083-H116).
*   **Aerospace:** Wing structures, fuselage components, and interior parts (e.g., specialized 2xxx and 7xxx series available on request).
*   **Construction:** Roofing, cladding, architectural trim, and road signs (e.g., 3003-H14).
*   **General Fabrication:** Machinery parts, tooling, and consumer goods.
